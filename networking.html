Networking

REINZ Centre of Excellence provides you with a centrally located and easily accessible networking space available for hire. With a fully integrated kitchen, it suits any type of function, from small intimate breakfast seminars to a larger evening networking functions.

For functions held in the Te Anau Cafe, the use of the kitchen is complimentarily. The kitchen is fully equipped with an oven, microwave, a fridge and a drink fridge.It is possible to have catering delivered and our staff is happy to recommend local catering providers.

Rotoma conference room

This event space is perfect when you are hosting a larger networking event. It is equipped with three large projector screens and a speaker system.

Te Anau cafe & kitchen

For a mingle networking event, the Te Anau café is the perfect space. With our fully equipped kitchen, you have a range of catering options to choose from.
